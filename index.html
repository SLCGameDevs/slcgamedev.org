Welcome to the SLC Game Devs!

We are a group of game developers, composers, artists, and more located in the Salt Lake City area. We have monthly in-person meetings that involve educational presentations, member game testing, gaming socials, and more! To become part of the community, join our Discord server to get to know us!

Meeting Time & Place

Normally, we meet at the Millcreek Public Library in the meeting room north of the cafeteria, from 3PM to 6PM on the last Saturday of the month. Please check our "Meetings" channel on the Discord server to verify before you attend, because sometimes we need to meet at other libraries and times.
